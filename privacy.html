2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Information

When you interact with our Services, we may collect personal information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be linked to you. The personal information we collect depends on the nature of your interaction with us and may include:

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your username, password, profile information, preferences, and any other information you choose to provide in your account settings. This information helps us personalize your experience and provide account-related functionalities.

Transaction Information: We maintain records of products you view, items you place in your cart, and purchases you make through our affiliate links. This includes details of the products, transaction amounts, dates of purchases, and referral data. This information enables us to track conversions, calculate affiliate commissions, and improve our product recommendations.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our Services, including pages visited, time spent on pages, navigation paths, frequency of visits, referring/exit pages, and other parameters about your device and internet connection. This data helps us understand user behavior and optimize our Services accordingly.

Device Information: When you access our Services, we automatically collect technical data about your device, including the type of device, operating system, browser type and version, language preferences, screen resolution, mobile network information, device identifiers, and other diagnostic data. This information is necessary to ensure proper functionality and compatibility of our Services.

Location Data: We derive your general location from your IP address. This information helps us provide regionally relevant content, ensure compliance with local laws, and detect potentially fraudulent activities. We do not track your precise GPS location unless you specifically grant us permission to do so.

Consent: In certain cases, we process your personal information based on your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time. This applies particularly to marketing communications, cookies that are not essential, and certain types of automated processing.

Legal Obligation: We may process your personal information to comply with legal requirements, such as tax laws, consumer protection regulations, data protection laws, or in response to lawful requests from public authorities.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Services employ cookies, web beacons, pixels, tags, scripts, and other similar tracking technologies to improve functionality and user experience. These technologies collect and store information when you visit or interact with our Services.

Essential Cookies: These cookies are strictly necessary for the basic functionality of our Services. They enable core features such as security, network management, account authentication, and remembering your preferences. You cannot opt out of these cookies as our Services cannot function properly without them.

Functional Cookies: These cookies enhance the functionality of our Services by storing preferences beyond what is strictly required. They remember choices you make, such as language preferences, font size, or region, and provide enhanced, personalized features.

Analytics Cookies: We use these cookies to collect information about how you use our Services, including which pages you visit most frequently, how you navigate through the site, and whether you encounter any error messages. The data collected is aggregated and used to improve how our Services work. We primarily use Google Analytics for this purpose, which processes your data according to its own privacy policy.

Advertising Cookies: These cookies track your browsing habits and activity to build a profile of your interests. They are used to deliver targeted advertising that is relevant to you. They also limit the number of times you see an ad and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. They are usually placed by advertising networks with our permission.

Cookie Management: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically delete existing cookies, block certain types of cookies, or set your browser to notify you when cookies are being placed on your device. However, removing or blocking cookies may impact your experience and limit certain functionalities of our Services. For more detailed information about cookies and how to manage them, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Do Not Track: Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Due to the lack of a common understanding of how to interpret these signals, our Services currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals.

Right to Access: You have the right to request information about the personal data we hold about you. Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate personal information we maintain about you. We will process such requests taking into account the nature of the information and the purposes of processing the information.

Right to Erasure: Under certain circumstances, you have the right to request deletion of your personal information from our records. We will comply with such requests unless retention is necessary to comply with legal obligations, detect fraud, enforce agreements, or protect our or another person's rights, property, or safety.

Right to Restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information under specific circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of your personal information or object to our processing.

Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit this information to another controller without hindrance, where technically feasible.

Right to Object: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information based on legitimate interests, including profiling. You also have the right to object to the use of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights. This means we will not deny services, charge different prices, or provide a different quality of service based solely on your decision to exercise your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section of this Privacy Policy.
